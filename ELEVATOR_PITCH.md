
# Really Simple Membership — Elevator Pitch

## The Value Proposition

* **FOR** club administrators, business owners, and membership-based organizations,
* **WHO HAS** the need to issue, update, and manage member credentials without the high cost of custom mobile apps or the waste of physical plastic cards,
* **Really Simple Membership**
* **IS A** digital membership management and mobile wallet pass platform
* **THAT** instantly delivers beautifully designed, interactive membership passes directly into members' native Apple Wallet and Google Wallet apps.
* **UNLIKE** traditional membership software that requires members to download a separate app or carry a plastic card,
* **THE PRODUCT** allows admins to visually design card layouts in real time, automatically triggers geofenced lockscreen notifications when members approach club locations, and pushes instant, remote card updates or revocations to all devices in one click.
