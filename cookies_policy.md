# Cookies Policy

**Last Updated:** February 16, 2026

## 1. What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and understand how you interact with the content.

## 2. How we use cookies
Really Simple Cards uses cookies for the following purposes:
- **Essential Cookies:** Necessary for the website to function, such as maintaining your login session.
- **Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand how users interact with cards and our dashboard, allowing us to improve the service.
- **Functional Cookies:** Remember choices you make (like language preferences).

## 3. Third-Party Cookies
We use third-party services like Clerk (for authentication) and Google Analytics. These services may set their own cookies to provide their functionality.

## 4. Your Options
Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:
- Block all cookies (this may prevent some parts of the site from working).
- Delete existing cookies.
- Set preferences for which cookies to accept.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at support@reallysimple.apps.
